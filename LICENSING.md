Cairn is offered under a **dual-licensing** model.

version 3.0** (see [`LICENSE`](LICENSE)). You may use, study, modify and
self-host it freely, **provided that**:

- derivative works stay under the AGPL, and
- if you run a **modified** version as a network-accessible service, you make
  the *Corresponding Source* of your modifications available to that service's
  users (AGPL **§13**).

Running an **unmodified** Cairn for yourself or your organisation has no extra
obligation beyond the AGPL.

## 2. Commercial license

If the AGPL's obligations don't fit your use case — for example you want to:

- embed Cairn (modified or not) in a **proprietary** product, or
- offer it as a **hosted / SaaS** service **without** publishing your
  modifications,

then a separate **commercial license** is available from the copyright holder,
which removes the AGPL copyleft/source-disclosure requirements.
